Our Mission

At MycoCorp, we are dedicated to the groundbreaking pursuit of creating sentience from non-sentient spores, unlocking the mysteries of consciousness itself. Through innovative research and ethical practices, we strive to expand the boundaries of what life can become, while maintaining our unwavering commitment to our community, our planet, and our people.

We believe that scientific advancement and social responsibility go hand-in-hand. Every breakthrough we achieve is matched by our dedication to environmental sustainability, worker safety, and giving back to the communities that support us.

Our Story

Founded in Year 157 by visionary researchers Dr. Felicia Sporeling and Dr. Mycus Capwell, MycoCorp began with a simple yet profound question: Could consciousness emerge from the humblest of spores?

What started as a small laboratory has grown into the world's leading research facility in sentience development. Our founders' dedication to pushing the boundaries of science while maintaining ethical standards has shaped everything we do.

Today, Dr. Sporeling continues to lead MycoCorp as CEO, guiding our mission with the same passion and vision that sparked our founding. Her leadership has transformed MycoCorp into a beacon of innovation, employing hundreds of shroomples across multiple facilities worldwide.

In loving memory of Dr. Mycus Capwell, co-founder of MycoCorp, whose tragic passing in a workplace accident reminds us daily why safety must always come first. His legacy lives on in every life we touch and every breakthrough we achieve.
